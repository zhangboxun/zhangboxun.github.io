About

(Work in progress)

Boxun Zhang is a data scientist. He’s currently leading the data science team at GoEuro/Omio, and worked previously at Spotify for several years. He has worked on various ML/data science problems: payment fraud detection, search result ranking, predicting user behaviors (e.g., retention and churn), and experimentation. Before joining the industry, he obtained his PhD from TU Delft, the Netherlands, where he studied the operations and user behavior in large-scale P2P systems.
